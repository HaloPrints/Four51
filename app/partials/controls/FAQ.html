Shipping Locations

We ship items via UPS, domestically and internationally. (May vary for some international orders.) UPS does not deliver to PO Boxes. We also offer flat rate USPS shipping rates. Domestic Orders-please note average delivery time is 7-10 business days.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs depend on the size and weight of your order. Please refer to the details within your shopping cart while making an order.

For International orders - the USPS shipping method does not include import or customs taxes if applicable. This is a common occurrence for international orders and unfortunately, we have no control over these fees or payment method.

If you are interested in placing a bulk order for your team/company, please contact us at shop@puppetlabs.com and we will work with you on a negotiated rate.
